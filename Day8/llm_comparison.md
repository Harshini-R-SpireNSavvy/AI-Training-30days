## Prompt Used
"Explain what is Artificial Intelligence"

---

## Response 1 — Basic Prompt
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. It includes tasks like problem-solving, decision-making, and language understanding.

---

## Response 2 — Detailed Prompt
Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the ability of machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. It includes technologies like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. AI is widely used in applications like chatbots, recommendation systems, and self-driving cars.

---

## Response 3 — Structured Prompt
Artificial Intelligence (AI) can be defined as:

1. Definition: Simulation of human intelligence in machines  
2. Key Areas: Machine Learning, NLP, Computer Vision  
3. Applications: Chatbots, Healthcare, Autonomous Vehicles  
4. Benefits: Automation, efficiency, accuracy
