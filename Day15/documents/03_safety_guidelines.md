# Laboratory Safety Guidelines

## Personal protective equipment
Safety goggles meeting ANSI Z87.1 must be worn whenever handling chemicals or operating the centrifuge. Nitrile gloves are mandatory for all wet chemistry benches.

## Chemical storage
Flammable solvents must be stored in the approved flammables cabinet only. Maximum container size on the bench is 500 mL unless a hot work permit is on file.

## Incidents
All spills and injuries must be reported to the lab supervisor within 15 minutes. The emergency eyewash station is located at the north end of Room B204.
