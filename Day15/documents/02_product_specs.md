# Product Specifications — Widget X

## Electrical
Widget X operates at a nominal voltage of 12 V DC. Acceptable input range is 11.4 V to 12.6 V. Peak current draw is 2.5 A during motor startup; steady-state current is 0.8 A.

## Environmental
Operating temperature range is 0 °C to 40 °C. Storage temperature is −10 °C to 60 °C. The device is not rated for outdoor use without the optional IP54 enclosure kit (part WH-X-ENC54).

## Warranty
The standard warranty period is 24 months from the date of purchase. Warranty claims require the original serial number and proof of purchase.
