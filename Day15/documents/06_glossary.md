# Glossary — Data & AI Terms (Internal)

## RAG
Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) combines search over a knowledge base with a language model so answers stay grounded in source documents.

## Embedding
An embedding is a dense vector representation of text. Similar meanings produce vectors that are close together in vector space.

## Chunking
Chunking splits long documents into smaller segments for indexing. Good chunk boundaries preserve semantic units such as paragraphs or sections.

## Hallucination
In this glossary, hallucination means the model stating facts not supported by retrieved context. Mitigation includes citations, confidence thresholds, and human review for high-risk answers.
