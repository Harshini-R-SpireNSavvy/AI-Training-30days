# Company Handbook — Acme Labs

## Remote work
All engineering staff may work remotely up to three days per week. Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 UTC. Managers approve the weekly schedule in the HR portal.

## Time off
Full-time employees accrue 22 paid vacation days per calendar year. Unused days roll over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next year. Carryover beyond that is forfeited unless an exception is approved by HR in writing.

## Security
Laptops must use full-disk encryption and a screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity. VPN is required for access to internal APIs from outside the office network.
