# New Hire Onboarding FAQ

## First day
Arrive at Building C reception at 09:00 on your start date. You will receive a badge, laptop, and instructions for IT setup. The default laptop image includes Python 3.11, Docker, and the company VPN client.

## Accounts
Create your SSO password before noon on day one using the link in the welcome email. Slack workspace invitations are sent automatically once SSO is active.

## Parking
Visitor parking is in lot P2. Employees use lot P5 with the hang tag issued by Facilities during week one.
