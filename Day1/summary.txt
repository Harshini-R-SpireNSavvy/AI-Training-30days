Machine Learning (ML):
Machine Learning is a way for computers to learn patterns from data and make decisions without being directly programmed. For example, it can be used to predict results, classify data, or recommend products.

Large Language Models (LLM):
LLMs are advanced AI models trained on large amounts of text data. They can understand and generate human-like text. Examples include chatbots that answer questions or help in writing.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation):
RAG is a method where the model first retrieves relevant information from a source and then generates an answer based on that. This helps in giving more accurate and updated responses.

Agentic AI:
Agentic AI refers to systems that can take actions on their own to complete tasks. Instead of just responding, they can plan, decide, and perform steps to achieve a goal.
